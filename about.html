About Us

Pop Anime Reviews was created by Tyler Friesen, Roger Pilon, and Edmund Viagedor.

This site was created as a way for us to provide our input on which popular animes are a must-watch and which animes are a miss. We provide quick, straight to the point reviews using a pros/cons approach in order to keep animes spoiler-free for the reader's sake! We also provide with our reviews a short clip or trailer for the anime for you to get an idea about what the anime is all about.

We will be updating and expanding our list of anime reviews, so in the meantime bear with us! Hope you all enjoy our site and feel free to 'like' us on Facebook and drop a comment on any of our reviews!
