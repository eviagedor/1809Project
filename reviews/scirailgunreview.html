A Certain Scientific Railgun Review by Roger Pilon

Pros :

  • Excellent animation.
  • Characters are quickly developed enough to see where their significance is in the story.
  • The characters are humorous.
  • The real world problems that some characters face make them relatable to many audiences.
  • There is a wide variety of powers and levels of powers which allow for unique characters to develop more clearly.
  • Story follows a set path that makes it easier to follow.

Cons:

  • The behaviors some of the girls display make it awkward, especially when you realize the girls are underage, even though they look to be around 17 to 18 years old.
  • If the viewer is too young or sensitive to graphic content, they will not like this show due to some fights as well as parts of the story line will be too intense for them.
  • Side characters become developed before they are needed for the story on occasion, which may throw the viewer off.

Rating: 6.5/10
