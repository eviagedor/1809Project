Naruto: Shippuden Review by Edmund Viagedor

Pros :

  • Very engaging and emotional story when the main story arc is being followed.
  • Lots of character development so you'll have an idea of where characters come from; physically and ideologically.
  • Fight scenes contain lots of action and is very fast-paced.
  • A variety of engaging characters so you will likely have more than one favorite character.
  • Story delves deep into Naruto lore so you can really understand how things in the Naruto world came to be.

Cons:

  • TOO MANY fillers! (Story arcs which deviate from the main story)
  • Fillers are inserted during a main story arc, and often run for many episodes disrupting the flow of the main story.
  • Animation can seem outdated at times, especially when you compare new episodes to older episodes.
  • Speaking of animation, during fight scenes animations can seem rushed and very clunky.

Rating: 8.5/10
