RWBY Review by Roger Pilon

Pros :

  • Everything is a gun.
  • The fight scenes are well worked.
  • There is a nice correlation between the names of the teams and the hidden themes.
  • The story line is decently worked so that each character has a unique and relevant story as to how or why they are there.
  • The world is very detailed story wise which allows for much to be learned about the world, which is covered in the side series called "World of Remenant".

Cons:

  • The animation is a little rough.
  • Some characters are a little out of place when it comes to what is going on.
  • New characters become important too soon as well as they are not developed at all before they become important.
  • Story line is about the whole world but the events only take place in one city.
  • While the story is good, the show skips occasionally to later parts too soon, which causes minor confusion at important times.

Rating: 7/10
